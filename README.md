# Numlock: Number Puzzle - Support

## About

Numlock: Number Puzzle is a timed number logic puzzle game where you combine tiles using arithmetic operations to reach a target number. Challenge your math skills, build combos, and compete on the leaderboard!

## How to Play

1. You're given a 4x4 grid of number tiles and a target number
2. Select two tiles, then choose an arithmetic operation (+, x, -, /)
3. The tiles combine into one with the result
4. Keep combining until you reach the target number
5. Beat the clock and build combos for higher scores!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I restore my purchases?**
A: Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases."

**Q: How does the Daily Challenge work?**
A: Each day a new puzzle is available for all players. Complete it to build your daily streak!

**Q: How do I access the leaderboard?**
A: Tap the leaderboard icon from the main menu. You'll need to be signed into Game Center.

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Yes! The core game works offline. Leaderboards and daily challenge syncing require an internet connection.

**Q: How do combos work?**
A: Complete rounds quickly in succession (within 1.5 seconds) to build a combo multiplier for bonus points.

## Privacy Policy

Numlock: Number Puzzle respects your privacy.

- **Data Collection:** We do not collect any personal data. Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
- **Game Center:** If you opt in, your scores are shared with Apple Game Center for leaderboard functionality. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
- **Ads:** The app may display ads provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may collect anonymized usage data as described in their respective privacy policies.
- **Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics services.
- **Children's Privacy:** We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
